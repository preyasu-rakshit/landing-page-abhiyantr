About Us

Founded by IIT Delhi alumni, Abhiyantr Robotics embodies the spirit of engineering ("Abhiyantr") with a vision rooted in India and ambitions that reach the globe. We’re building agile, snake-like robots tailored for inline inspection of small-diameter, unpiggable pipes—typically hidden in critical assets like supercritical boilers, economisers, and shell-and-tube heat exchangers.

Our mission: to bring regular, precision inspection and cleaning within reach, unlocking major environmental and safety benefits. Industry studies show that pigging and other robotic cleaning methods use less fluid and generate more manageable, concentrated waste—improving energy efficiency and mitigating environmental burden.

Moreover, our robotic NDT approach dramatically reduces human exposure to hazardous conditions—from confined spaces and toxic fumes to high-temperature hazards—by enabling remote inspection of inhospitable areas.
